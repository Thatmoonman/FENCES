WELCOME TO FENCES

In FENCES, you are tasked with getting your piece (BLUE) from one end of the board to the other.

Simple, right? Well your opponent (RED) is trying to do the same!

So how do you stop them?

WITH FENCES OF COURSE!

You can place a fence to block their movement, but beware!

Where you block them, you also block yourself,

so don't FENCE yourself in!

    How To Play:
  • Click your piece to select it, you can move to any adjacent square or "jump" your opponent.
  • Fences cannot be crossed, so place them wisely!
  • Valid moves will be highlighted in green, click a highlighted square to move there.
  • To Place a fence: simply select the BLUE block off screen.
  • Select a fence posts where you would like the fence to start, and then a post next to it.
  • Click & Drag or swipe to move the camera around and zoom and out.
  • Spacebar returns the camera to it's starting position.

GOOD LUCK!
