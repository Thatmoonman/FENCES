WELCOME TO FENCES

In FENCES, you are tasked with getting your piece (BLUE) from one end of the board to the other.

Simple, right? Well your opponent (RED) is trying to do the same! So how do you stop them?

WITH FENCES! You can place a fence to block their movement, but beware!

Where you block them, you also block yourself, so don't FENCE yourself in!

    How To Play:
  • Click your piece to select it, you can move to any adjacent square that doen't cross a fence or "jump" your opponent.
  • Valid moves will be highlighted in green, click a highlighted square to move there
  • To Place a fence: simply select the BLUE block off screen,
  • Select a fence posts where you would like the fence to start, and then a fence post next to it.

    • GOOD LUCK!
